How We Work

From sheet metal to a running unit.

Every unit is built end-to-end inside our Jeedimetla workshop — designed, rolled, welded, balanced, tested and dispatched without sub-contracting the critical work.

  1. 01

    Design & sizing

    Pick the right impeller, casing and motor for your CFM, pressure and temperature.

  2. 02

    Fabrication

    Plate cutting, 3-roll bending, full-penetration welding, MS and SS construction.

  3. 03

    Balancing

    Static and dynamic impeller balancing for vibration-free, long-life operation.

  4. 04

    Testing

    No-load run, current draw and noise check before paint and packing.

  5. 05

    Delivery & install

    Crane lift, transport and on-site commissioning anywhere in South India.

About Sahasra

A workshop you can walk into.

We are a family-run fabrication unit in IDA Jeedimetla — the heart of Hyderabad's industrial belt. We build what we sell, and we stand behind every unit.

For more than {{YEARS_IN_OPERATION}} years we've been fabricating industrial blowers and ventilation systems out of our workshop in Jeedimetla. We started small — repairing units for neighbouring factories — and grew the way good workshops do: one referral at a time.

Today we build everything from compact direct-drive blowers to large twin-inlet centrifugal units with belt drive and engineered impellers, ship across South India, and keep customers running with quick service calls when something needs attention.

  • Owner-run, no middlemen — speak to the person who builds the unit.
  • Heavy-gauge MS & SS construction, dynamically balanced impellers.
  • Walk into our shop in Jeedimetla — see your unit before it ships.
  • Quick-turn repair and breakdown service across Hyderabad.
